Water Drying Service Agency Since 2023

WaterSec was born from the desire of five engineers who wanted to tackle water stress in Tunisia and the MENA region. Indeed, it is considered one of the most effective ways to change consumer behavior and address water stress issues.

Starting from the observation that we can only optimize and manage what we measure, monitoring consumption was chosen as the solution to kickstart the project.
